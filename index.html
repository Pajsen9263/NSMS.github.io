    What's Adminator?

    Adminator is an assistant from NSMS that helps you manage your school's administrative tasks. Schools can use this system to manage and compile grades, health infomation, payroll infomation, and even student and staff attendance. Adminator also helps parents access their children's dashboard to pay fees and check their children's performace!
